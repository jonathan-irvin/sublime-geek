The Life of Mr. Roundy

Mr. Roundy was a small, chubby man who loved CSS3. Of particular affection was the multiple background image module, which at the untimely event of his passing was supported only by Safari.

This is the story of his life.

As a side note, Mr. Roundy was an exceptional badminton athlete. He practiced rigorously in the evenings and on weekends.
